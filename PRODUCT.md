# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Seno's friend group (Avani, Pringle, Big T, Dih, Ishan, Evie, Hema, Yuvi, Shriya, Manahil, Gabby, Ayaan) — primarily experienced as a hackathon demo on mobile. Secondary audience: anyone who finds the demo funny. Users are 18–22 year olds who already know the in-jokes; the app rewards close reading.

## Product Purpose

SenoedIn is a parody LinkedIn built around Seno's cat Arthur and Seno's actual interests: indoor climbing, Minecraft/Let's Plays, and music compatibility. Five core pages — cat post feed, climbing route portfolio, game audit log, music swipe network, DMs — each modeled on a real LinkedIn feature but filled with absurdist corporate-speak about naps, territory disputes, and diamond incidents. Zero real API keys required for demo mode; every AI feature falls back to deterministic parody content. The app exists to be shown off in a hackathon and immediately make everyone laugh.

## Brand Personality

Playful · Cozy · Irreverent. Warm and fun, leans fully into the parody without being frantic. The humor is deadpan and specific — LinkedIn vocabulary applied to cat behavior with complete sincerity. The tone never winks; it just reports the facts.

## Anti-references

- Generic LinkedIn clone: the whole point is that it should feel nothing like actual LinkedIn
- Dark SaaS dashboards with cold blue/gray palettes: no clinical, enterprise-feeling UI
- "AI-generated" uniformity: identical card grids, eyebrow labels on every section, hero metrics with gradient accents
- RGB rainbow gamers aesthetic: the dark theme should feel premium and editorial, not like a Discord clone
- Minimalism for its own sake: this app should have warmth and personality, not austere empty space

## Design Principles

1. **Specific over generic** — Every element earns its place. If a design choice could appear on any dark-mode app, it's wrong for SenoedIn.
2. **Warmth in the dark** — Black backgrounds should feel cozy, not cold. Garnet and gold are the fire; use them to make the dark feel inhabited.
3. **The joke is always in the copy, never the layout** — Layout should be composed and readable so the absurdist text lands without interference.
4. **Earned delight** — Personality comes through small, specific moments (a garnet notification dot, a cat quote with a gold border, a friend comment that clearly knows the inside joke) not from decorating everything at once.
5. **Mobile-first without apology** — Designed for a phone held in one hand at a hackathon. The experience should feel tight and intentional at 390px, not like a desktop layout that happens to scroll.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No formal WCAG target stated. Maintain readable contrast on all text (≥4.5:1 for body, ≥3:1 for large/bold). Reduced motion: respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for swipe animations and scan line effects.
